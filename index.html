The Beagle Blog

The Beagle id a breed of small scent hound,similar in appearance to the much
larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as
beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the
beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural
imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent
and is a popular pet dut to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health
problems.

Trivia

  • Height - 13-16 in (33-41cm)
  • Weight - 22-25 lb (10.0-11.3kg)
  • Coat -Short haired,hard coat of medium length
  • Colour -Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  • Life span - 12-15 years
